1. Get the application URL by running these commands after install:

  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -l app={{ include "proman.name" . }} -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 3000:3000

Then open http://localhost:3000

2. If you uploaded an image tar to TrueNAS SCALE, load it onto the cluster nodes or use a local registry and update `values.image.repository` accordingly.
